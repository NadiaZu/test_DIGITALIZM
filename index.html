Mastercard ®Thanks Medics For Taking Care!

The medics who work at the Georgian clinics, where the COVID-19 infected patients are being treated, will use free transportation from Mastercard

video list of clinics

About Campaign

Healthcare workers are the modern heroes in times of pandemic. they have to go to work every day and do their best for the wellness of their cities, countries, people and the world. Mastercard supports the healthcare workers and provides them with everyday comfortable rides.

Within 3 big cities of Georgia (Tbilisi, Kutaisi, Batumi), we have 9 clinics, where the COVID-19 infected patients are being treated. The medics of those clinics will use the free transportation of Mastercard and they will use the Bolt app for that.

Those, who want to use the free transportation and already has the Bolt app, should select MC For Medic as a payment method and activate the service. Those, who don’t have the app, can download it from this page. For detailed information, you can also see a video-instruction on this page about how to download the app and use the free transportation. Here you can also see the list of participant clinics.

Considering the State of Emergency, the app will be available for use from 7:00 am to 8:00 pm.
